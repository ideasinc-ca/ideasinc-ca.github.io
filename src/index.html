International Derivatives Expertise and Systems

Solutions for derivatives and quantitative risk management

IDEAS (International Derivatives Expertise and Systems) is a multidisciplinary firm researching global financial market structure and regulations, with deep experience in OTC derivatives markets and trading.

Founded in 2013, we provide sophisticated solutions in derivatives valuation, portfolio optimization, and regulatory capital analysis. Operating from Toronto's financial district, we serve major financial institutions across US/CA/UK/EU jurisdictions.

Leadership

Founder & CEO

Fixed Income & Rates
